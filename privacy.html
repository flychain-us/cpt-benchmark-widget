Flychain ("we," "our," or "us") operates the ABA Rate Benchmark tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Information you provide:

Automatically collected:

  • Browser type and device information

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide rate benchmark comparisons

Data Storage

Your email and usage preferences are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. When you submit your email, it is sent to our secure systems via encrypted channels.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services (such as Zapier) to process form submissions. These services adhere to industry-standard security practices.

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting us at privacy@flychain.us.

Contact Us
